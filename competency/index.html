Link vs Dr. Robotnik

FSDI 103 Competency Report

By Todd Mickel, Cohort 18

Welcome to my Link vs Dr. Robotnik activity. This isn't really a game, per se, as there is no objective and no way for the player to 'win' or 'lose.' This is intended to display my grasp of the basic concepts of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. It serves as my Competency Report for FSDI 103 at San Diego Global Knowledge University.

I created two versions of this activity:

  • Version 1 meets the requirements of the FSDI 103 assignment. You have the option of attacking with either Link or Dr. Robotnik. The activity will keep track of each character's hit points and display a 'game over' screen when one of them runs out of hit points. You will then have a chance to reset the activity and try it again.
  • I decided to try to change the parameters up a bit for Version 2. This one is a little more like a game. When Dr. Robotnik appears, the 'Attack with Link' button will appear at a random spot along the bottom of the game and you will have a short period of time to click the button. If you don't, Dr. Robotnik will attack Link.
